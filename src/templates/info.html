This web application is designed to estimate and visualize blood alchohol concentration (BAC). Calculations are based on the Watson formula, as described in the paper: "Posey D, Mozayani A. The estimation of blood alcohol concentration : Widmark revisited." (DOI: 10.1385/FSMP:3:1:33)

How to use the app

To begin using the app, update your information on the profile page (these are needed for the BAC calculations). Then you can add drinks on the front page and view your estimated BAC plot. The BAC plot displays your estimated blood alcohol concentration in permilles as a function of time. Please note that the plot only displays the past and coming 12 hours, so any drinks that fall outside this timeframe won't be visible in the plot.

To use the room functionality, simply go to the rooms page and create a new room. Other users can join your room by using the room name. The rooms contain a plot displaying the estimated BAC curves of all room members.
